Is Solar a Good Investment?

For most U.S. homeowners, solar panels deliver a 10–20% annual return on investment — significantly better than a savings account or even many stock market investments, when you factor in the guaranteed electricity savings and rising utility rates.

The 30% Federal Tax Credit

The Residential Clean Energy Credit reduces your federal tax bill by 30% of your solar installation cost. On a $20,000 system, that's $6,000 off — bringing your net cost to $14,000. This credit is available through 2032 and does not have an income cap.

Factors That Affect Solar ROI

  • Electricity rates: Higher rates = faster payback
  • Net metering: Full-retail net metering maximizes ROI
  • Financing: Cash purchase returns more than a loan
  • System size: Sized to your actual usage avoids over/under-building
  • Panel quality: Better panels degrade slower (0.3–0.5%/yr)
