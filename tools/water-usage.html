Where Does Household Water Go?

The average American uses about 82 gallons of water per day indoors. Toilets are the biggest indoor user (~24%), followed by showers (~20%) and faucets (~19%). Outdoor watering can double total household usage in summer months.

Top Ways to Save Water

  • Install WaterSense-labeled fixtures (toilets, showerheads, faucets)
  • Fix leaks — a dripping faucet wastes ~3,000 gallons/year
  • Run dishwasher and washing machine only when full
  • Use a smart irrigation controller to avoid overwatering
  • Consider drought-tolerant landscaping in dry climates
