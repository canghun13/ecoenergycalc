Which Green Upgrades Have the Best ROI?

Not all green upgrades are created equal. Some pay back in months; others take a decade. Here's a quick guide to help you prioritize:

Best ROI Upgrades

  • LED lighting: Payback in months. Low cost, high savings, no brainer.
  • Smart thermostat: $100–250 cost, $150–200/year savings. Under 2 years payback.
  • Heat pump water heater: ~$1,200 after IRA credit, saves $300+/year. ~4 year payback.
  • Insulation + air sealing: $1,500–5,000, saves 15–30% on H/C. 3–8 year payback.
  • Solar panels: $14,000 after credit, saves $1,200–1,500/year. 8–12 year payback, 25-year profit.

IRA Incentives (2023–2032)

The Inflation Reduction Act provides generous credits: 30% for solar/wind/batteries, up to $2,000/yr for heat pumps, up to $1,200/yr for insulation/windows/weatherization. Stack these with state/utility rebates for even faster payback.
